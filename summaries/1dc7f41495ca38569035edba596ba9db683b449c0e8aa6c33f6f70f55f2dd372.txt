The article discusses a speech by Mike Burgess, the director-general of Australia's domestic spy agency, in which he highlights that Australia is facing foreign interference from three or four countries, including some that are traditionally considered friends. Burgess emphasized the need for Australians to be vigilant and recognize the threat posed by foreign interference to the country's sovereignty and national security. He also emphasized the role of the Australian Security Intelligence Organization (ASIO) in countering these threats and protecting Australia's interests.
