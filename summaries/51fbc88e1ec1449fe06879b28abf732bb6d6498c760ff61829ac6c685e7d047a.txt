Lachlan Morton, a cyclist, completed an incredible 14,200km ride around Australia in record time. The journey took him 45 days, surpassing the previous record by 25 days. Morton faced intense weather conditions, sleep deprivation, and physical challenges during his epic ride. He documented his journey on social media and received support from fans and followers along the way. Despite feeling physically exhausted at the end, Morton expressed gratitude for the experience and the opportunity to showcase the beauty of Australia through his ride.
